Beautiful, Functional Spaces Designed for You

Every space has a story and at Astrakat Designs Limited, we bring that story to life with a perfect balance of style, craftsmanship, and practicality.

Our team specialises in interior design, custom furniture, joinery, and modular solutions that transform houses into homes and spaces into experiences. From modern kitchens and wardrobes to bespoke fittings and layout planning, we focus on every detail to ensure seamless functionality and timeless aesthetics.

We believe great design isn’t just about how it looks , it’s about how it feels and works for you.

What Our Expert Thinks

Great design begins with understanding people - their stories, their habits, and their dreams. At Astrakat Designs, we believe every space should reflect the personality and lifestyle of the people who live in it. Our approach goes beyond aesthetics; it’s about creating harmony between form and function. From the first sketch to the final finish, we focus on details that transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. For us, interior design is not just about what you see. it’s about how it makes you feel the moment you walk in.
